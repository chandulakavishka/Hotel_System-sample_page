Hotel Riu Sri Lanka

The RIU Sri Lanka opened in 2016 and offers a 24h All Inclusive service. The hotel is located in the south of Sri Lanka, directly on Ahungalla beach. The RIU Classic Hotels offer comfortable rooms and excellent facilities with great leisure and relaxation facilities, as well as international gastronomy. Enjoy a relaxing holiday - whether alone, with family, partner or friends! The location is ideal because of its proximity to Ahungalla (500 m), Benota (15 km) and 130 km away from the Colombo Int’l Airport (CMB). The hotel has 501 comfortably furnished rooms and offers many modern amenities. Relax in the spa area or get in shape at the gym. 3 outdoor pools, 1 children’s pool. RiuLand Kids Club (ages 4-7 & 8-12 years) and for adults, day and evening entertainment. For lovers of gastronomy, live cooking stations at our main restaurant. Asian and Italian specialty restaurants and 4 bars and “Pacha” discotheque

Le Grand Galle

Ekho Safari Tissa

A rejuvenating waterfront retreat- EKHO Safari occupies a prime position in Tissamaharama. Scenically located by the ancient Tissa Lake, the property provides undisturbed views of the lake and beyond. EKHO safari traces its roots back when it started operation in 1967, now restored to a comfortable and beautifully laid lake view hotel. All 47 deluxe rooms and 3 suites are completed with modern comforts and overlook the tranquil lake. Wake up to watch the flurry of early morning bird activity and take a dip in the pool for a refreshing start to the day. Remember to try out the local favourite, “Kalu Dodol” made to the age old recipe of Tissamaharama
